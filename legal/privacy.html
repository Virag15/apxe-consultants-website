We keep what we collect to the minimum needed to do our job. We don’t sell data, we don’t run ad networks, and we only keep records as long as we actually need them.

1. Who we are

Apxe Consultants (“Apxe,” “we,” “our”) is a business advisory firm based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website, client engagements, and direct communications.

2. What we collect

We collect only what’s needed to respond to your enquiries, deliver our services, and comply with our legal obligations.

Information you give us

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, phone, company, and the message you write.
  • Booking information when you schedule a discovery call through our calendar tool (Calendly).
  • Client engagement data shared during the course of advisory work (covered by a separate engagement agreement).

Information collected automatically

  • Basic analytics: pages visited, approximate location (country/city), device and browser type.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. See our Cookie Policy for detail.

3. How we use it

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  1. To respond to contact forms and calendar bookings.
  2. To deliver advisory services agreed in writing.
  3. To improve this website based on aggregate usage patterns.
  4. To send you information you’ve explicitly asked for (for example, a specific case study).
  5. To meet our legal, tax, and regulatory obligations in India.

What we don’t do: we don’t sell your data, we don’t share it with ad networks, and we don’t send unsolicited marketing. If you didn’t ask for it, you won’t receive it from us.

4. Sharing & disclosure

We share personal information with a small number of third parties only when necessary:

  • Service providers who help us operate (email hosting, analytics, calendar booking). Each is contractually bound to handle data on our instructions only.
  • Legal authorities when we are legally required to disclose information.
  • Professional advisors (auditors, legal counsel) under standard confidentiality obligations.

We do not transfer personal data outside India without appropriate safeguards under applicable law.

5. Cookies & tracking

This site uses a minimal set of first-party cookies for essential functionality and anonymised analytics. We don’t use third-party advertising cookies. See the Cookie Policy for the full list.

6. Data storage & retention

Information is stored on servers located in India and, in some cases, via cloud services with infrastructure in the EU or Singapore (covered by standard contractual clauses).

Retention periods:

  • Contact form enquiries: retained for 24 months, then deleted.
  • Client engagement records: retained for 7 years to comply with tax and professional obligations.
  • Analytics data: retained in aggregate form only, for up to 26 months.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw consent at any time for any processing that relies on it.
  • Complain to the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email sales@apxe.in. We’ll respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be flagged at the top of this page and communicated to active clients directly.
